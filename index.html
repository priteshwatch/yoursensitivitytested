The Manifesto

Why We Do This

01

We're Not Getting Paid

There's no paycheck. No sponsorship deal. No agent waiting in the wings. We do comedy because standing in front of strangers and trying to make them laugh is the most alive you can feel without a parachute.

02

We Don't Care What You Think

Actually, that's a lie—we care a little. But not enough to stop. If your feelings get hurt, that's between you and your therapist. We're here to test boundaries, including our own.

03

We Take Fun Seriously

We're not professional comedians. But we prepare like we are. We write, we rewrite, we bomb, we learn, and we get back up. The only difference between us and the pros? We don't have to pretend to like our booking agent.

04

Your Sensitivity? Consider It Tested.

The name isn't a warning—it's a promise. Comedy is supposed to make you uncomfortable sometimes. If every joke lands softly, nobody's actually saying anything. We'd rather be honest than polite.
